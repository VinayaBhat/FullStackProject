About Us

Everytime we want to watch a movie or TV show we find it hard to get suitable streaming links. This website was designed to make it easier for the users to find movie information and get the streaming links.

Movie information displays the movie/tv show posters, information about the actors, directors, production, writers, year, Box Office Collection, Plot, Genre,Ratings and Runtime. Clicking on streaming links displays the links of websites when the user can watch the movie/ tv show.

The latest page is dedicated to display the posters of movies currently running in theatres along with their titles.

The Upcoming page shows the posters of upcoming movies with their titles and release date.

If you have any queries please go to our contact page and submit the contact form and we will get back to you.
